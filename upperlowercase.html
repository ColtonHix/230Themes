Changing the hashbang: the hashbang is the top line of the program, and should be changed based on where the perl.exe executable is saved on your computer

Windows example: #C:\Dwimperl\perl

Mac OSX example: #!/usr/bin/perl

Run this program using the command: perl UpperLowerCase.pl
